Breakfast

Breakfast is served in the Dining Room between 8.30 am and 9.30 am offering a range of hot dishes from the grill.
Our delicious Full Grill will set you up for the day ahead. If you prefer something lighter simply choose other options from the menu.
To make your stay as pleasurable as possible, please advise in advance of any special dietary requirements.
